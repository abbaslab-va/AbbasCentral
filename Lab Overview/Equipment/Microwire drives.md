One way we collect [[Electrophysiology]] data is through custom fabricated microwire arrays, consisting of tungsten wire filaments pinned into an electrical interface board with gold pins. These wires are then organized into bundles and fixed in place according to the needs of the experiment. Some drives will also have larger, high impedance LFP wires that can be implanted in disparate brain regions. The process of constructing these drives is covered in more detail in [[Drive Fabrication]].
